'Mr. Robot's' Rami Malek Set to Play Freddie Mercury in New Queen Biopic
November 11, 2016

Mr. Robot‘s Rami Malek has been casted as Freddie Mercury in an upcoming Queen biopic. Directed by Bryan Singer, the movie has been in development for almost a decade under the eye of Graham King of GK Films, who will be producing. 20th Century Fox and New Regency will co-finance the film, with a script written by Anthony McCarten. Original Queen members Brian May and Roger Taylor have been brought on as music producers for the project. What do you think about this casting choice?

adidas Concept Store No74 Is Redesigned in Berlin
November 11, 2016

adidas‘ concept store in Berlin has reopened following a redesign. The store, called No74, inspired by industrial themes juxtaposed against minimal and modular elements. Clothing racks are constructed from welded metal, while seating and displays are made out of pink and purple blocks. No74 Berlin will continue to carry its exclusive collection of adidas footwear and apparel, housing everything from Y-3 to Yeezy and Pharrell.

The high-end store on Melrose Avenue was transformed into a ’70s-themed living room, complete with wood-paneled walls and shag carpeting.
